  • 24 June 1987 - Lionel Messi is born in Rosario, Argentina

  • 30 March 1994 - Messi joins Newell's old boys

  • 16 October 2003 - Messi makes his first appearance

  • 24 July 2005 - Messi Inks his first contract

  • 17 May 2006 - Messi plays in the champions league

  • 23 August 2008 - Messi Plays in the summer olympics

  • 27 May 2009 - Messi wins the 2009 UEFA champions league with Barcelona
