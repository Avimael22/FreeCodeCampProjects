Play freely with ideas

A demonstration of the new MIDI tools

Reshape MIDI patterns

Apply a variety of simple or complex variations to your MIDI clips with new MIDI Transformations. Add ornaments and articulations, draw acceleration and decelaration curves, connect successive notes and chords, simulate the strum of a guitar, and more..

Generate Inspiration

Conjure up melodies, chords and rhythms with the help of new MIDI generators. Set constraints and let your chosen generator create playful and original ideas for you to develop and turn into your own.

Do more with Max for Live

Try out additional Max for Live Transformations and Generartors that give you deeper way of working with MIDI, or go even further by building new ones.

See how to work with tuning systems in live

Enhance and explore tuning

Follow your preferred tuning systems of try different ones more easily. Work outside 12-tone equal temperament, and use your tuning with live's devices as well as any MPE - capable plugin or hardware.

A quick look at the keys and scales feature.

Stay in Key

Transform or generate ideas within the confines of the selected scale, or sync the scale of MIDI devices and instruments to the clip being played.

Edit MIDI more easily

Use keyboard and mouse operations to edit MIDI in new ways. Split a note in two or chop it into several parts. Select a set of notes and join them together, or make them fill a selected time range. Plus, you can access to more notes from the notes utilities panel on the left, such as fit to scale, Humanize, Add Intervals and more.

Modulate more flexibility

Stay in control of a parameter, even after its been mapped to a modulation source; Max for live devices like LFO, shaper and envelope follower now let you freely adjust a destination that's being modulated.

Find inspiration

Start playing with any of live's new instruments and effects and you'll soon find new textures and techniques to spark further experimentation

Lose yourself in Meld

Live's new MPE capable synthesizer is designed for sound variety, playfullness and character. Equipped with two independent oscillators and an extensive modulation matrix, Meld excels and textural soundscapes, harmonal and atonal sounds, and rhythimic drones.

Meld's macro oscillators provides simple access of cmoplex waveforms made by various synthesis methods. Quickly dial up subtractive, FM, granular, and other sources. Quantize the oscillators to a scale and even use the LFOs from once oscillator to modulate the other.

Program Meld's dual oscillators with dedicated LFOs and an extensive modulation matrix

Each of meld's oscillator presets comes with two engines parameters, carefully chosen for maximum sound design versatility.

Listen to more sounds from Meld.

Add color with Roar

Bathe your sound in subtle warmth - or break it down with wild distortion. Live's new coloring and saturation device is made up of three saturation stages for serial, parallel or even mid/side and multiband configurations. It's built-in compressor and feedback routing give your even more ways to shape your sound, and its modulation section lets you add dynamic, evolving saturation and color.
